Data: facts or figures from which conclusions may be drawn

temporal: relating to time

data science vs. computational science

empirical: based on experience or observation rather than theory

computation data scientist tasks
- collecting and categorizing large data sets
- cleaning and validating data

common questions that a computational data scientist will ask
- what % of users come back to our site?
- which city is better for opening new office?
- which products usually bought together?

computational and data science pipeline
acquire -> cleanse -> explore -> model -> present

session: a period devoted to a particular activity

mystery: something that is difficult to understand or explain

demystify: make (a difficult subject) easier to understand
